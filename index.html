I was born and raised in Upstate New York, but my real roots reach to the Mediterranean. I owe my love for warm weather and Italian food to my ingrained hertiage - it's in my blood!

My passion: I LOVE outdoor physical activity - it is an outlet for my restless tendencies and exploring different places both locally and around the world ignite my creativity. There's something about being out of the house that really inspires me and enriches my life.

A fun fact: I didn't find my true "home" until I was 16 on my first visit to New York City as a teen. For this misunderstood outsider from a small town, New York's towering buildings and busy streets wrapped around me like a hug and I had this overwhelming feeling of safety and calm. It was a life-changing moment that gives me chills to this day!
